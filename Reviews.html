Reviews

Mark Nutter - 2 reviews - US - Rated 4 out of 5 stars - Mar 20, 2024

"I initially signed up for a two-year plan with automatic payments, and I must say, the services provided during that time were quite satisfactory. Upon reaching the end of my plan, I promptly communicated via email and phone that I wouldn't be renewing my membership. However, there seemed to be a misunderstanding, and automatic withdrawals continued from my bank account. Fortunately, I was able to rectify the situation, albeit with some effort. While my experience with the cancellation process wasn't as smooth as I hoped, I can attest to the quality of the gym itself. Despite the minor hiccup, I found the facilities to be well-maintained and the equipment up to par. However, I must acknowledge that there are other gyms like Planet Fitness that offer competitive rates and a wider range of amenities. Overall, I appreciated my time at the gym and the value it provided during my membership. While there were some challenges with the cancellation process, I believe they can be addressed with improved communication procedures. Despite my experience, I wouldn't hesitate to recommend the gym to others, as the quality of service and facilities are commendable."

Date of experience: March 20, 2024
